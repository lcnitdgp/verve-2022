Literati

Do you want to weave your thoughts into a constellation? Would you like to weigh your pen against others? The Literary Circle is here with the annual creative writing competition, Literati, a long haul from all the essays you've written so far. This is the chance to unleash the inner spark of creativity that's buried within an engineer, waiting to be brought out and create it's own mystical realities and fantasies. Let them actualize in a pen that has the potential to inscribe the future, to bend waves, and animate what lies inside of you.
Date: 30th March, 2021.
Time: 6 PM.
For more details, contact:
Sakshi: 7718233559
Anwesha: 7604098828

CONVINCIO

False hope. False promises. But, Real Emotions. Real persuasion. All of us want to make people follow our path, and believe everything we say. Well, that isn't a pipedream anymore. This is your chance to experience the heavenly power of words, in a scale never heard of before. The potential to break the wall of human consciousness and emerge victorious in a battle of the fittest, is within you. With enthralling events round the corner, get ready to shine and bring out the chieftain in you.
CONVINCIO. VERVE XVI.
And most importantly, No Prerequisites, at all.
Date: 10th April.
Time: 3:00 PM.
Save the date, and check in, on time.
