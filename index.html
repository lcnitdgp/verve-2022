Literrati

The most extravagant and glamorous fashion show has been the opening night of Incandescence, NIT-Silchar. The vision of glitterati starts with raising the curtains and setting the onset of diversity in fashion. Over the years our show has ensured new designers, the charisma to take the leap of faith and show the world the portion of creativity and innovation. We believe fashion is not about just wearing an incredible creation. It’s about how we emblazon our passion. This year we have an optimist, a young talented designer “KAMAL LOCHAN BORAH” who aced the glamour fashion world with his creative collection. He started his career as a designer for labels like BESTSELLERS, JACK & JONES, ZARA, TOM TAYLOR, SELECTED, etc. His collections exhibits a distinctive style of both the Indian and western silhouette of both the cultures and can be ascertained in his own designing label “VEDICAA”

Thundermarch

Thundermarch, the annual rock music festival of NIT Silchar is one of the biggest events held in the North-eastern region. The festival makes everyone delve into the sea of rock music and bang their heads to the metallic tunes. Thundermarch has been drawing a huge crowd and been successful ever since it began in 2008. It has previously seen performances by popular bands like Boomerang, Demonic Resurrection, Chronic Xorn etc.
